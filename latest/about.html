What is Trapyz

Planetworx Technologies is a industry leading provider of IoT technologies for real-time locationcontext intelligence for Retail, Aviation and Hospitality sectors on SaaS model. Trapyz Locate and Trapyz Connect, our RTLS and PLS solutions provides much-needed location data analysis to better target customer segments and serve real-time personalised notifications.


We leverage smart hybrid sensors/beacons with support for multiple industry protocols with a easy-to-deploy-and-use model. Trapyz empowers enterprises to aggregate, analyse and leverage large amounts of location data to gain real-time insights and design marketing interventions that help drive footfalls and conversions completing the Online to Offline digital experience.
